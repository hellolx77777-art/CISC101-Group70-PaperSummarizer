# Module 6: Equation Explainer (Student-Designed Module)

## Purpose
Identify equations in the paper and provide plain-language explanations.

## Responsibilities
- Detect mathematical equations (inline or block)
- Translate equations into step-by-step explanations:
  - What variables mean
  - What the equation is used for
  - Why it matters in the research
- Provide audience-adaptive explanation:
  - Expert → technical detail
  - Layperson → intuitive explanation

## Inputs
- Paper text containing equations

## Outputs
- Equation explanations for inclusion in summaries or glossary
