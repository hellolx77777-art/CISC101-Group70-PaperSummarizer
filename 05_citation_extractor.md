# Module 5: Citation Extractor

## Purpose
Extract citation information from the research paper and flag inconsistencies.

## Responsibilities
- Scan paper text for citation patterns:
  - APA, MLA, numeric brackets, inline author-year formats
- Extract each citation into a structured list
- Identify:
  - Missing references
  - Incomplete references
  - Citation inconsistencies

## Inputs
- Full research paper text

## Outputs
- Citation list for the final output
- Citation-related warnings
